Product Review : Milo

by Olayemi Michael 2020 July 08
Product name: Nestle Milo
Category: Beverages
Manufacturer: Nestle Inc.

Looking for a product that not only refresh you but also revitalize you and supply quality nutrient, then milo may be what you are looking for. I could remember an afternoon in my hometown, I just felt like drinking something and being a nutritionist, I will prefer both calorie and nutrient-dense product. I thought of what to take when I realized Milo is the best short and it satisfies my eagerness to drink something. The taste was nice and the thickness was awesome (actually the thickness is based on the volume of water used in preparation). Milo is a chocolate drink with the following ingredients: Milk powder, barley malt extract, sugar, cocoa, sodium phosphate, dicalcium phosphate, soy lecithin, vitamin A palmitate, thiamine hydrochloride,ferrous fumarate, artificial flavor. The ingredients are rich in protein, carbohydrate, and micronutrients which make it a better choice over soft drinks. Milo can be enjoyed both hot and cold.
